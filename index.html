Palestinian refugees

Palestinian refugees are people who lost both their homes and means of livelihood as a result of the 1948 Arab–Israeli conflict[139] and the 1967 Six-Day War.[140] The number of Palestinians who fled or were expelled from Israel following its creation was estimated at 711,000 in 1949.[141] Descendants of these original Palestinian Refugees are also eligible for registration and services provided by the United Nations.
